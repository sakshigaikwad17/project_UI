Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

About

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a great warrior and visionary ruler. He was known for his military genius, administrative skills, and progressive ideas.

Early Life

Born in 1630 at Shivneri Fort, he was inspired by his mother Jijabai and tutor Dadoji Konddeo. He displayed leadership at a young age.

Achievements

Shivaji Maharaj founded the Maratha Empire and fought against the Mughal Empire with strategic brilliance. He was coronated in 1674.

Legacy

His ideals of Swarajya, self-rule, and justice continue to inspire people in India and beyond. His forts, navy, and warfare methods are studied today.
