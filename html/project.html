Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

About

Shivaji Maharaj was born on 19 February 1630 at Shivneri Fort. He was a brave warrior and the founder of the Maratha Empire in western India.

Key Facts

  • Born in Shivneri
  • Coronated as Chhatrapati
  • Passed away at Raigad Fort
